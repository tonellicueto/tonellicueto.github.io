Teaching

My teaching experience consist of approximately 200 hours distributed among six university-level courses. Below, a simplified account of my teaching experience can be found. Since this is a very limited experience, I am planning to extend this experience in the next years if possible.

  • Technische Universität Berlin
    • Effective Algebraic Geometry Seminar, organizer (together with Peter Bürgisser and Alperen A. Ergür), SuSe 2018. (syllabus)
  • University of the Basque Country
    • Groups of units in complete discrete valuations rings (Minicourse), lecturer, February 2014.
    • “$\mathsf{P}$ vs. \(\mathsf{NP}\) and related topics” Seminar, organizer (together with Luis Martínez) and main lecturer, WiSe 2012-13 and SuSe 2013.
  • Centro de Estudios INTEC
    • 1st year Mathematics for students of the B.Sc. Chemical Engineering at the University of the Basque Country, private remedial teacher, SuSe 2014.
    • 1st year Linear Algebra for students of the B.Sc. Mathematics at the University of the Basque Country, private remedial teacher, WiSe 2013-14.
    • 2nd year Linear Algebra for students of the B.Sc. Mathematics at the University of the Basque Country, private remedial teacher, WiSe 2013-14.

For more details regarding my teaching experience, see the corresponding section of my CV.

Communication/Popularization of Mathematics

My activity in the popularization of mathematics has been concentrated during my period as editor of the mathematics students' magazine $\pi kasle$. After that, I have been mainly inactive in this role, although I may want to come back to it at some moment in the future.
