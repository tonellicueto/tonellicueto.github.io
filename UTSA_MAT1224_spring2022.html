Bibliography and Supplementary Materials

The course will be based mainly in the following textbook:

  1. E. Herman, G. Strang, W. Radulovich, et al. Calculus (Volume 2). OpenStax, 2016.
    url: https://openstax.org/details/books/calculus-volume-2.

The following supplementary material covers sometimes topics beyond the scope of our course, but the parts that correspond to our course can be useful to supplement the textbook, lectures and exercises of the course.

  1. B. Demidovich, ed. Problems in Mathematical Analysis. Trans. by G. Yankovsky. Mir, 1970.
    url: https://archive.org/embed/DemidovichEtAlProblemsInMathematicalAnalysisMir1970
  2. R. Ghrist. Calculus: Single Variable by Professor Robert Ghrist.
    url: https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLKc2XOQp0dMwj9zAXD5LlWpriIXIrGaNb
  3. R. A. Silverman. Essential calculus with applications. Dover Publications, 1989.
